Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

"The Missile Man of India"

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, known as the Missile Man of India, was an aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He inspired millions with his vision for India's future and his unwavering belief in the power of youth and education.

Major Achievements

  • Played a leading role in India's civilian space program and military missile development.
  • Known as the "Missile Man of India" for his work on the development of ballistic missiles and launch vehicle technology.
  • Served as the President of India from 2002 to 2007.
  • Recipient of Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award.

Inspirational Quote

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."
